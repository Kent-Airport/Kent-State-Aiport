FAQs

Where is the Aiport Located?

The Kent State Airport is located at 4020 Kent Road, Stow Ohio 44224.

How many planes do you have?

We have around 30 planes of varies different models.

Do you have flights all times of the year?

Yes, we offer flights during all times of the year including holidays weather permitting.

Do you offer refunds

Yes, depending on the situation we do offer ticket refunds if your flight is cancelled at least 2 weeks prior to your flight or if we cancel your flight for a reason like mechanical issues or weather. You can contact us for further details.
